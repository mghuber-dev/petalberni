Brand refinement

Refined Mountain Paw logo variations

Five professionalized variations based on the selected Mountain Paw direction. The recommended final mark is Variation 1 because it has the clearest paw at small sizes, the warmest personality, and the best balance of local landscape and pet-directory recognition.

Recommended

1. Teal & Gold Mountain Paw

Deep teal gives the mark trust and a Vancouver Island coastal feel, while warm gold makes the paw friendly, readable, and memorable. This is the strongest all-purpose logo for web, favicon, social profiles, print, and merchandise.

2. Ocean & Fresh Green

Brighter and more outdoorsy, with an energetic blue-green palette. Good for a cheerful local directory, though slightly less distinctive than the gold-paw version.

3. Teal & Sky

Soft, open, and coastal. The sky-blue base feels welcoming online and keeps the mountain silhouette calm and professional.

4. Monochrome Utility

A one-colour version for stamps, embroidery, favicons, print constraints, and future merchandise where colour reproduction may be limited.

5. Harbour Sunset

The warmest option, pairing teal with a soft sunset accent. Friendly and local, but a little more playful than the selected core identity.
