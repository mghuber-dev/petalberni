Emergency Pet Care

There is currently no dedicated 24-hour emergency veterinary hospital in Port Alberni.

If your pet requires urgent medical attention outside the hours of your regular veterinarian, call an emergency clinic before beginning your drive. Clinics may have changing hours, emergency capacity limits, or temporary staffing changes.

What Should I Do?

  1. 1Stay calm and keep your pet as comfortable and safe as possible.
  2. 2Call your regular veterinarian if they are open.
  3. 3If they are closed, phone one of the emergency clinics below before travelling.
  4. 4If your pet has difficulty breathing, has been hit by a vehicle, is actively bleeding, has collapsed, is having seizures, or has ingested a toxin, seek emergency veterinary care immediately.
