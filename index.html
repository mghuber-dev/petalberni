Why PetAlberni exists

A practical home base for local pet care.

Finding pet services should not mean digging through scattered posts, old search results, or word-of-mouth threads. PetAlberni is being built to make local pet care easier to find, easier to compare, and easier for the community to keep up to date.

Local first

Focused on Port Alberni and the Alberni Valley—not a generic national directory.

Easy to use

Clear categories and plain language help people get to the right service quickly.

Community supported

Local suggestions will help shape which businesses and organizations are added.
